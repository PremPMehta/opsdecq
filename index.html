Frequently asked questions

Who is OpsDecQ for?

OpsDecQ is built for internal teams who need a structured way to manage tasks, track project progress, and stay accountable without admin-heavy complexity.

Is OpsDecQ secure?

OpsDecQ is a role-controlled portal with secure login, session handling, and restricted actions to ensure users only access what theyre permitted to.

Will my team need training to start using it?

Not really. OpsDecQ is designed to be self-explanatory. Most users can log in, see their assigned tasks, and start updating progress immediately

How is OpsDecQ different from tools like Asana or Monday?

OpsDecQ keeps things focused: task execution, project visibility, deadlines, and updates, without the overload of complex workflows, setups, or admin-heavy controls.

Can I track tasks in different formats?

Yes! You can manage tasks using a Kanban board view, a list view, and a calendar view depending on how you prefer to plan and execute.

What happens when a task becomes overdue?

Overdue tasks are automatically flagged, making it easy to spot delays early and prioritize what needs attention right away.

Can tasks be broken down into subtasks?

Yes. Subtasks help break work into smaller actions, and task completion is controlled so the parent task cant be marked complete until subtasks are finished.
