What is Opsdecq AI and how does it work?

Opsdecq AI is an advanced AI-powered chatbot designed to help businesses engage customers, boost sales, and automate support. It works on both WhatsApp AI and your website, offering a seamless way to interact with customers in real time.

With Opsdecq AI, you can:

  • Automate customer conversations on WhatsApp and your website.
  • Recover abandoned carts with personalized WhatsApp messages.
  • Run WhatsApp marketing campaigns to drive sales.
  • Answer customer queries instantly with AI chatbots.
  • Increase conversions by providing immediate support and recommendations.

Whether youre running an e-commerce store, offering services, or need 24/7 customer assistance, Opsdecq AI simplifies communication and enhances customer engagement.

How does Opsdecq AI help recover abandoned carts?

Opsdecq AI allows you to send automated reminders to customers who left items in their cart. Through WhatsApp, you can send:

  • Personalized abandoned cart recovery messages to encourage customers to complete their purchase.
  • Exclusive discounts or time-limited offers to create urgency.
  • Follow-up messages to re-engage potential buyers.

These messages are customizable to match your brands tone and can significantly increase recovered sales.

Can Opsdecq AI be used for marketing campaigns?

Yes! Opsdecq AI allows you to send:

  • Personalized promotions directly to customers via WhatsApp.
  • Product recommendations based on user behavior.
  • Newsletters and announcements to keep customers engaged.

By reaching customers on WhatsApp, where they are most active, you can increase engagement and conversions effortlessly.

How does it enhance customer service on my store?

Opsdecq AI acts as your 24/7 automated assistant, instantly answering customer inquiries and guiding them through the buying process. It can:

  • Provide product recommendations based on customer needs.
  • Answer FAQs automatically.
  • Offer order tracking updates via WhatsApp.

How do I integrate Opsdecq AI with my Shopify store?

Setting up Opsdecq AI is quick and easy:

Step 1: Sign Up for Opsdecq AI
Create an account on Opsdecq AI and access your dashboard.

Step 2: Connect WhatsApp Business
Follow the guided setup to link your WhatsApp Business account. Youll need a Meta Business Manager account and a verified business phone number.

Step 3: Install Opsdecq AI on Your Website
Add the chatbot to your website by copying a simple code snippet into your sites settings.

Step 4: Customize Automations
Set up automated responses, abandoned cart recovery, and marketing campaigns using Opsdecq AIs intuitive interface.

Step 5: Start Engaging Customers
Your AI chatbot is now live! Start responding to customers instantly and converting more leads into sales.
